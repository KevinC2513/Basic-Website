TRUE
Founded
We were founded in 2003. One day our founder Kevin Chen was doing homework when his legs once again bumped up against the legs of the tables. That’s when he decided he had enough! He was adamant on creating the next best table and saving legs from annoying furniture for people around the world!
Mission
While Kevin’s Goal is to create comfortable tables, we aim to provide the best tables for all consumers. We aim to create the best tables for the luxery consumer with stylish designs as well as the most compact tables for people like Kevin!
So Far
Over Fifty thousand pieces of furniture sold
25 awards for outstanding durability, comfort, and price
Employing over 200 employees
80% of staff stay over 5+ years
Donate 10% of our profits to charity annually
